Historical daily sales data used to train time-series forecasting models for business planning.

  • Date range: 3 years of daily data (2019–2023)
  • Frequency: Daily — 365 rows per year
  • Target column: sales

🧹 Preprocessing

Fill missing date gaps and cap outlier spikes before feature creation.

  • Reindex to daily: asfreq('D')
  • Fill gaps: interpolate(method='time')
  • IQR bounds: Q1 − 1.5×IQR → Q3 + 1.5×IQR
  • Winsorise extreme spikes — clip not drop
  • Final missing count: 0

✂ Chronological Train / Test Split

Split in time order — no random shuffle — no data leakage.

  • Sort by date ascending — strict chronological order
  • Train: first 80% of rows (2019–2022)
  • Test: last 20% of rows — unseen future data
  • Scale: StandardScaler fit on train only, apply to test
